Synopsis

Some time has passed since the battle on Mount Olympus. Lyzer is working with The Brotherhood to ound up the last of Alex's men while searching for Fliz and Flamme. Soon, Lyzer and Hiel receive word about sightings of two women using powers located in the Eastern Hemisphere. Upon meeting the two women, Lyzer and Hiel separate to discover many secrets about themselves and the women they've confronted. Tasked with new knowledge and understanding, they work together to defeat the women and rescue Fliz and Flamme.
